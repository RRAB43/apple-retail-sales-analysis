⚠️ Analyst Note
The uniform ~82% decline across 8 international stores in 2023 is statistically unlikely to reflect true performance. This flags a potential data pipeline issue or store reclassification — a real-world finding that demonstrates mature analytical thinking.
✅ Signal: Q4 Seasonality Strong
Every year shows Q4 uplift of +34% vs Q2 average. iPhone launch cycles drive September spikes. Predictable and actionable for inventory planning.
📈 Signal: 2022 Was an Outlier Peak
The 2022 Q4 $85M quarter was 2× the historical average. Likely driven by iPhone 14 launch + post-COVID pent-up demand. Don't model 2022 as the new baseline.
🔴 Risk: 2021 Q1 Data Gap
Q1 2021 shows only $3M — almost certainly missing data. Any forecast model trained on this dataset needs to handle this outlier or it will underweight the trend.
